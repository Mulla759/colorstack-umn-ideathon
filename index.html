CODE OF CONDUCT

Rules & Regulations

EXPECTATIONS

  • Respect all participants, judges, and organizers
  • No pre-built projects or prior work allowed
  • All code and assets must be created during the event
  • Open-source libraries and APIs are permitted

SUBMISSIONS

  • Presentations must not exceed 5 minutes
  • All team members must contribute to the pitch
  • Judges’ decisions are final
  • Projects may be showcased by ColorStack UMN
